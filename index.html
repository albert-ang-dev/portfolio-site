< ABOUT ME >

I’m Albert, a web developer based in New York with a passion for building websites that actually work for your business — not just look good.

I specialize in working with small local businesses who need a real online presence without the big agency price tag. Whether you’re starting from scratch or your current site is overdue for an upgrade, I’ll handle everything from design to launch so you can focus on running your business.

I’m also a firm believer that there’s no need to reinvent the wheel. Great website templates already exist — professionally designed, battle-tested, and ready to go. My job is to take those foundations and customize them to fit your brand, your content, and your goals. You get a great-looking site faster, at a lower cost, without sacrificing quality.

I build with modern tools like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and WordPress — choosing whatever fits your needs and budget best.
